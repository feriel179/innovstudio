About The Studio

A Paris practice built around light, material, and restraint.

Innovstudio was founded in Paris on a simple belief: that a building should feel inevitable, as if no other shape could have worked as well. We work across architecture and interiors as one continuous discipline, carrying a single idea from the first sketch through to the last door handle.

Every project starts with the site, the light, and the people who will actually live in the space — not with a style we're trying to repeat.
